What We Do:
Pinot's Palette is the upscale paint and sip studio in Denver. Here you can Paint, drink and have fun every time you come! We generally paint on canvas while you sip on your favorite beverage from our bar. At our studio, everyone is an artist - no experience necessary!

Our artist instructors will guide you step by step in creating your very own masterpiece during our two and three hour classes. You can follow their direction, but you're also welcome to let your creative juices flow and do your own thing on the canvas! Our classes are anything but boring! Good music, good drinks and good people will make your experience one you won't forget!

We'd love to help you plan and celebrate your next night out, date night, birthday, bachelorette party, sprinkle shower, or anniversary! We also specialize in team building, corporate and special events.

We have two rooms in our studio. Our main studio will seat 48 - 52 people very comfortably. You won't feel crammed in our space! Our private party room, Studio 5280, will hold up to 20 people. We have a bar and serve wine, beer and non-alcoholic drinks. You are not allowed to bring in your own beverages. You are, however, allowed to bring in your own food. Both spaces are available for private parties any day of the week and at the time that works for you and your private event. Check our calendar and call to confirm availability.

About Private Events:
Hosting a private event at Pinot's is a great way to ensure that you and your guests will have a memorable, professional and stress-free experience. We will gladly help you take care of the details of planning your event with us. Additional event planning services available for additional fees.

We have two rooms in our studio and can accommodate any type of event - even during our public class times. We'd love to help you celebrate birthdays, sprinkle showers, bachelorette parties, kid's parties & anniversaries! We specialize in team building, corporate and special events. Costs are $35/person for a 2-hour class and $45/person for a 3-hour class. Extra time can be added at the rate of $75 for every 30 minutes and custom paintings can be created for your group for an additional fee. There is a minimum of a three-week lead time for custom paintings.

Private parties can be held anytime, based on availability. Because we have two rooms, we can host private parties during our public class times. We require a minimum of 10 painters (or 10 paid seats) to hold a private party. You are welcome to bring in your own food/snacks for your event and purchase drinks from our well-stocked bar.

Private Event Deposit:
There is a $250 deposit required for private events held in our studio. This deposit is fully refundable if you meet the minimum requirements for your event. The deposit can also be fully or partially applied to the total cost of the event.

Supplies Included:
We provide everything you need to make your masterpiece - the canvas, easel, brushes, paints and aprons. We have a well-stocked wine/beer and non-alcoholic beverage selection available for purchase. We can accommodate most special requests with enough warning and strive to make your event special and memorable!

Cancellation:
In order to receive a refund on your deposit, you must cancel your event a minimum of one week (seven days) prior. If you cancel less than a week before, we retain your deposit.

Kid's Parties:
We offer a number of kid-friendly painting selections that are perfect for birthdays, play groups, family fun days and more. Parties for kids under 12 years of age are $25 per painter. Parties for kids 13+ are $35 per painter. Both require a 10 painter minimum.
